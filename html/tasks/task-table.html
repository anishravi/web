How long does the course take
  • Business careers are jobs you can get after completing a business degree. Depending on your specialization, your career options can expand beyond the business sector to provide business-focused roles in other industries.
  • Corporations, universities and nonprofits seek business professionals to serve in various roles, such as marketing, accounting and finance. In each specialization, you can choose from numerous jobs, allowing you the freedom to find a career best suited for your particular skill set and lifestyle.
  • A business degree makes most graduates qualified to work within most of these specialized fields. In a business program, you learn the requisite critical thinking and analytical skills necessary to succeed in any career.
 Earning a business degree typically takes two to six years. The amount of time you can expect to invest in getting your business degree depends on the type of degree you're pursuing. For example, earning an associate degree typically takes two years, while earning a bachelor's degree may take four years.
